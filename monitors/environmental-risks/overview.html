    Analytical approach

    ERM shows how environmental pressure moves through political, economic, and security systems rather than staying inside a policy silo.

    • Treats transmission into conflict, governance, food, and infrastructure as central.
    • Chronic stress and acute events are analysed together but not conflated.
    • Structural risk remains visible even when no single event dominates the week.
    Cross-monitor connections
    • SCEM — Resource stress and displacement intensifying fragile theatres.
    • GMM — Insurance stress, food prices, and supply shocks translating into macro consequences.
    • WDM — Environmental shocks amplifying governance stress and legitimacy challenges.
