What the AI Governance Monitor Tracks

The AI Governance Monitor is a 16-module weekly intelligence publication covering AI capability, regulation, investment, military AI, ethics, science, and governance. It is published every Friday at 09:00 UTC by Asymmetric Intelligence at asym-intel.info/monitors/ai-governance/.

The monitor applies a primary-sources-first standard. Every item requires a direct link to a lab research blog, arXiv preprint, official regulatory text, court filing, government gazette, or official changelog. Tier 2 and Tier 3 sources are only used when no primary source exists, and are flagged accordingly.

An item is included if and only if: it is new within the 7-day window; a senior professional in the target audience would want to know it; it has a primary source link; and it is not duplicated within the same report. There are no arbitrary item caps.
